Hi there, I'm Adam Riggall

I'm a software engineer with a side of DevOps. I work at Nike on the NIKEiD engineering team. I'm passionate about building unique web experiences powered by cloud services (which can be continuously deployed easily and reliably). For more about my work life, check out my resume.

My dissertation applied machine learning methods to neuroimaging data to understand how the brain performs short-term memory. In the process I developed new analytical approaches, interactive visualizations, and challenged a few long-held theories. For details, take a look at my CV.

When I'm not writing code, I'm likely sailing. Being on the water is a great passion of mine, as a lot of my side projects will attest.
